# Jairosoft - Code of Business Conduct

## 1. Introduction

At Jairosoft, we are committed to conducting our business with **integrity**, **transparency**, and **responsibility**. This Code of Business Conduct serves as a guide for all employees, contractors, and partners in upholding ethical and professional standards in every aspect of our operations. Adherence to this Code ensures that we foster a culture of trust, accountability, and excellence across all business activities.

## 2. Compliance with Laws and Regulations

Jairosoft complies with all applicable local, national, and international laws and regulations governing our business activities. Employees and partners must adhere to all legal requirements related to their roles, including but not limited to:

### Key Compliance Areas:
- **Data privacy and cybersecurity regulations** - Ensure the secure handling of sensitive information.
- **Intellectual property laws** - Respect proprietary software, patents, trademarks, and copyrights.
- **Anti-corruption and bribery laws** - Refrain from engaging in bribery or unethical business practices.
- **Fair competition laws** - Conduct business with integrity and avoid unfair competitive advantages.
- **Labor and employment laws** - Adhere to fair employment standards and workplace rights.

## 3. U.S. Government Compliance Policies

Jairosoft conducts business with the U.S. government and its agencies and adheres to strict regulatory and ethical standards, including:

### 3.1 Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) & Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS)

Jairosoft ensures compliance with FAR and DFARS when engaging in government contracts, including:

- **Procurement Integrity** - Prohibiting unfair competitive practices.
- **Ethical Bidding & Pricing** - Ensuring transparent and fair contract negotiations.
- **Security & Data Compliance** - Following CMMC (Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification) for defense contracts.

### 3.2 False Claims Act (FCA) Compliance

Employees and contractors must **never**:

- **Submit false or fraudulent claims to the U.S. government.**
- **Engage in misrepresentation of products, services, or contract obligations.**
- **Ignore compliance requirements in federal contracts.**

**⚠️ Warning**: Violations of the False Claims Act can result in significant penalties, including civil fines and criminal prosecution.

### 3.3 Anti-Kickback Act & Bribery Prevention

Jairosoft **strictly prohibits**:

- **Kickbacks, bribes, or gifts** to government officials or representatives.
- **Improper influence** in government contracting decisions.
- **Hiring conflicts of interest**, such as former government employees violating post-employment restrictions.

### 3.4 Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA)

Jairosoft adheres to the FCPA, ensuring that:

- **No bribes or improper payments** are made to foreign officials.
- **International business dealings** are conducted ethically.
- **Accurate financial records** are maintained for transparency.

### 3.5 Whistleblower Protections & Reporting Violations

- Employees are encouraged to report fraud, misconduct, or regulatory violations under the **Whistleblower Protection Act**.
- Jairosoft **prohibits retaliation** against employees who report concerns in good faith.

**Reports can be made through**:
- **Direct supervisors or managers**
- **HR or the Compliance Department**
- **Anonymous reporting channels** (where available)

### 3.6 Export Control & Trade Compliance

Jairosoft complies with U.S. export laws, including:

- **International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR)**
- **Export Administration Regulations (EAR)**
- **Sanctions under the Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC)**

Employees must ensure proper licensing before transferring technology, software, or data internationally.

## 4. Ethical Business Practices

Jairosoft is committed to ethical business practices in all our dealings. Employees and partners must:

- **Conduct business honestly and transparently.**
- **Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts promptly.**
- **Reject bribery, corruption, and unethical influence.**
- **Engage in fair competition and maintain respect for competitors.**

If employees encounter unethical behavior, they are encouraged to report it to management or the compliance team.

## 5. Confidentiality and Data Protection

Jairosoft values data security and privacy. Employees must:

- **Protect confidential company and client information.**
- **Comply with all relevant data protection laws and internal security policies.**
- **Avoid sharing proprietary information with unauthorized parties.**
- **Adhere to cybersecurity protocols to prevent data breaches.**

## 6. Respect in the Workplace

Jairosoft is committed to fostering a respectful, inclusive, and safe workplace. Employees must:

- **Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and professionalism.**
- **Avoid discrimination, harassment, and bullying.**
- **Promote diversity and equal opportunities.**

## 7. Quality, Innovation, and Continuous Improvement

As an AI-first company, Jairosoft is dedicated to:

- **Maintaining high product and service quality.**
- **Encouraging innovation and efficiency.**
- **Supporting customer satisfaction.**

## 8. Environmental and Social Responsibility

Jairosoft supports sustainability by:

- **Reducing environmental impact.**
- **Engaging in corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives.**
- **Supporting ethical sourcing.**

## 9. Health, Safety, and Security

Employees must:

- **Follow workplace safety regulations.**
- **Maintain secure and safe work environments.**
- **Protect company property and information.**

## 10. Responsible Use of Technology and Social Media

Employees must:

- **Use company resources responsibly and for business purposes.**
- **Avoid sharing confidential information online.**
- **Report cybersecurity threats immediately.**

## 11. Reporting Violations and Whistleblower Protections

Employees can report violations through:

- **Supervisors or HR**
- **Anonymous whistleblower hotlines**

Jairosoft **prohibits retaliation** against employees who report ethical concerns.

## 12. Enforcement and Disciplinary Actions

Violations of this Code may result in:

- **Warnings or suspensions.**
- **Termination for serious offenses.**
- **Legal action for fraud, corruption, or misconduct.**

## 13. Commitment to Continuous Improvement

Jairosoft reviews and updates its policies to align with evolving business, regulatory, and technological standards.

**By adhering to this Code of Business Conduct, we affirm our commitment to ethical excellence, compliance, and responsible corporate citizenship.**
