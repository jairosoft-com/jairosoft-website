# Style Guide

This style guide defines the **visual and interaction standards** for the enterprise website, ensuring consistency across all components, pages, and experiences.

---

## 1. Brand Colors

**Primary Colors**  
- Red: `#e50106` (Primary CTA, highlights, brand emphasis)  
- Black: `#000000` (Primary text, contrast)  
- White: `#FFFFFF` (Backgrounds, negative space, contrast)  

**Secondary Color**  
- Steel Blue: `#0078D4` (Trust signals, secondary actions, hover states)  

**Neutral Palette**  
- Dark Gray: `#333333` (Headings, body text on light backgrounds)  
- Light Gray: `#F5F5F5` (Backgrounds, dividers, secondary surfaces)  

**Usage Rules**  
- **Red**: primary buttons, links, CTAs.  
- **Blue**: secondary accents, hover/focus states.  
- **Black/Gray**: typography.  
- **White**: dominant backgrounds for clarity.  

---

## 2. Typography

**Primary Typeface**  
- DM Sans (or substitute: Segoe UI, Helvetica, Arial)  

**Weights & Usage**  
- Regular: body copy  
- Medium: secondary headings, buttons  
- SemiBold: primary headings, key highlights  

**Hierarchy**  
- H1: 36–48px, bold, line height 1.2  
- H2: 28–32px, medium/bold, line height 1.3  
- H3: 20–24px, medium, line height 1.4  
- Body: 16–18px, regular, line height 1.5  
- Caption: 12–14px, regular, muted color  

**Rules**  
- Left-aligned text is default.  
- Maximum line length: ~70 characters.  
- Consistent line height for readability.  

---

## 3. Layout & Grid

- **Grid System**: 12-column responsive grid.  
- **Baseline**: 8px spacing system.  
- **Whitespace**: generous spacing between sections.  
- **Alignment**: strict left and vertical alignment for order.  
- **Breakpoints**:  
  - Mobile: ≤600px (single column)  
  - Tablet: 601–1024px (2–3 columns)  
  - Desktop: ≥1025px (full grid)  

---

## 4. Buttons

**Primary Button**  
- Background: Red (#e50106)  
- Text: White (#FFFFFF)  
- Hover: Slightly darker red  
- Disabled: Light gray background, muted text  

**Secondary Button**  
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)  
- Border: Blue (#0078D4)  
- Text: Blue (#0078D4)  
- Hover: Blue background, white text  

**General Rules**  
- Minimum padding: 12px vertical, 20px horizontal  
- Minimum width: 120px  
- Consistent border-radius (4–6px recommended)  

---

## 5. Navigation

- **Header**: White background, black text, sticky on scroll.  
- **Logo**: top-left, always clickable to homepage.  
- **Menu Items**: uppercase or title case, evenly spaced.  
- **Active Item**: underlined or highlighted in red.  
- **Mobile Nav**: collapsible hamburger menu, full-screen overlay or slide-out.  

---

## 6. Cards & Content Blocks

- Background: White (#FFFFFF)  
- Border/Shadow: Subtle gray divider or light shadow  
- Padding: 16–24px  
- Internal structure: Image → Title → Body → Link/Button  
- Hover: slight elevation or background tint (blue/gray)  

---

## 7. Forms

- **Layout**: single-column preferred  
- **Labels**: always visible, left/top aligned  
- **Input Fields**:  
  - Background: White (#FFFFFF)  
  - Border: 1px solid #ccc  
  - Focus: border turns Blue (#0078D4)  
- **Validation**:  
  - Error: Red border + helper text  
  - Success: Green check or confirmation  

---

## 8. Motion & Animation

- **Timing**: 200–300ms for most transitions  
- **Easing**: ease-in-out for natural feel  
- **Use Cases**:  
  - Hover states (buttons, cards)  
  - Dropdowns and modals (slide/fade)  
  - Error feedback (gentle shake)  
- **Rules**:  
  - Motion must enhance clarity, not distract.  
  - Respect reduced-motion accessibility preferences.  

---

## 9. Accessibility

- Minimum color contrast: 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text.  
- Text resizable up to 200% without breaking layout.  
- All interactive elements keyboard-accessible.  
- Visible focus indicators (blue outline or glow).  
- Alt text for all non-decorative images.  

---

## 10. Imagery & Iconography

- **Imagery**: high-resolution, professional, authentic (avoid stock clichés).  
- **Style**: modern, sharp, high-contrast.  
- **Icons**: consistent stroke weight and size, preferably line style.  
- **Placement**: icons always paired with text labels when critical.  

---

## 11. Modularity & Scalability

- Components designed as **self-contained modules**.  
- Reusable across different templates and pages.  
- Named consistently for both design and CMS use.  
- Built with responsiveness and editor flexibility in mind.  

---

## 12. Personalization & Editor Experience

- Components must support multiple content variations.  
- Provide defaults/fallbacks when data is missing.  
- CMS names should be clear (e.g., “Hero Banner – Image Left”).  
- Previews available for personalization variants.  
- Documentation for editors included in the design system.  

---

# Summary

This style guide ensures **consistency, clarity, and trustworthiness** for the enterprise website.  
It is tightly aligned with the **Design Principles** document and provides tactical rules for daily execution.
